Privacy policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Parknav we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it. We can’t stand services that ask you for things like your gender or income level for no apparent reason.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of one of our services.

If you have questions about deleting or correcting your personal data please contact our support team (contact@parknav.com)

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Parknav collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Parknav's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Parknav's visitors use its website. From time to time, Parknav may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Parknav's websites choose to interact with Parknav in ways that require Parknav to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Parknav gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for a Parknav subscription to provide an email address. In each case, Parknav collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Parknav. Parknav does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Parknav may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites and mobile applications. For instance, Parknav may monitor the most popular parking spots. Parknav may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Parknav does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Parknav discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Parknav's behalf or to provide services available at Parknav's websites and mobile applications, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Parknav's websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Parknav will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Parknav discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Parknav believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Parknav, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of a Parknav subscription or service and have supplied your email address, Parknav may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with Parknav and our products. We primarily use our various product blogs to communicate this type of information, so we expect to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Parknav takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Parknav uses cookies to help Parknav identify and track visitors, their usage of Parknav's website, and their website access preferences. Parknav visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Parknav's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Parknav's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Business Transfers

If Parknav, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Parknav goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Parknav may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.
